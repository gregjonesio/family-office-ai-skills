# Principal Weekly Brief

A skill that turns the week's scattered inputs — calendar, open decisions, investment updates, operational issues, travel, and follow-ups — into a single, concise briefing a family office principal can read in minutes and act on.

## What it does

Consolidates raw weekly inputs into a structured brief with an executive summary, decisions needed, upcoming meetings, investment items, operational issues, risks or delays, suggested follow-ups, and a list of anything still missing.

## Who uses it

Chiefs of staff, executive assistants, and operators who prepare a recurring briefing for a principal.

## How to use it

1. Gather your inputs for the week (see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md) → *Inputs*). Provide only sanitized material.
2. Invoke the skill with your inputs (see the *Example request* in [SKILL.md](SKILL.md)).
3. Review the draft. **Verify every fact, figure, date, and name against source** before sharing it with the principal.
4. Resolve anything listed under *Open questions / missing information*.

## What it will not do

- It will not invent meetings, figures, or context.
- It will not make investment, legal, tax, or compliance recommendations.
- It will not decide anything or send anything.
