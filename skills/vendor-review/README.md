# Vendor Review

A skill that turns vendor material — a proposal, renewal notice, service-issue summary, or performance update — into a structured review that helps an operator decide how to handle the relationship.

## What it does

Produces a review: vendor overview, current relationship, key terms, service issues, cost considerations, risks, questions for the vendor, and a recommended process next step.

## Who uses it

Operations leads, chiefs of staff, and executive assistants managing vendors and service providers.

## How to use it

1. Gather the vendor material and relationship context (see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md) → *Inputs*). Provide only sanitized material.
2. Invoke the skill (see the *Example request* in [SKILL.md](SKILL.md)).
3. Review the output and **verify the terms and pricing** against the actual documents.
4. Use the *Questions for vendor* to drive the conversation; route any contractual questions to counsel.

## What it will not do

- It will not approve, sign, renew, or terminate anything.
- It will not declare a price fair or unfair without a basis, or invent comparables.
- It will not provide legal advice on the contract.
