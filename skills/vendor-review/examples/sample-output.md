## Questions for vendor

1. What exactly is included in the "expanded coverage," and what changed from the current scope?
2. What are the SLAs for response and outage resolution, given last year's two prolonged outages?
3. What justifies the 20% increase, and is a 12-month term still available?
4. What are the termination provisions and the exact auto-renewal notice deadline?
5. Can the disputed AV invoice be resolved before any renewal is signed?

## Recommended next step

Do not sign within the 14-day window on the current information. Request the *Questions for vendor* answers (scope, SLAs, termination, and resolution of the open invoice), and initiate a benchmark of the category against at least one or two alternative providers before deciding whether to renew, renegotiate, or replace. Route the contract terms to counsel if a multi-year commitment is contemplated.
