# Meeting Prep Pack

A skill that assembles a structured meeting brief from prior notes, attendee details, agenda, open issues, and desired outcomes — so a principal or operator walks in fully prepared.

## What it does

Produces a concise pack: meeting objective, attendees, relevant background, open items, suggested questions, desired outcomes, and materials to review beforehand.

## Who uses it

Executive assistants, chiefs of staff, and operators preparing principals (or themselves) for meetings.

## How to use it

1. Gather the meeting basics, attendees, agenda, prior notes, and desired outcomes (see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md) → *Inputs*). Provide only sanitized material.
2. Invoke the skill (see the *Example request* in [SKILL.md](SKILL.md)).
3. Review the draft and **verify attendee details and prior-context facts** against source.
4. Resolve anything flagged under *Open questions / missing information* before the meeting.

## What it will not do

- It will not invent attendee backgrounds or prior history.
- It will not make recommendations or commit the office to a position.
- It will not draft or send communications to attendees.

## Files
