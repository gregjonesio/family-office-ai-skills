# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This is a professional, open-source project for family office and wealth management
operators and the AI builders who serve them. We expect everyone who participates —
in issues, pull requests, and discussions — to be respectful, constructive, and
professional.

## Expected behavior

- Be courteous and assume good faith.
- Keep discussion focused on the work: skills, documentation, safety, and usefulness.
- Give and receive feedback graciously and specifically.
- Respect confidentiality. Never post confidential, client-specific, or personal
  information in any issue, pull request, or comment.
- Uphold the repository's conservative safety posture in your contributions and reviews.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or demeaning comments.
- Sharing others' private information without permission.
- Posting confidential or proprietary information.
- Deliberately introducing misleading content, unsafe instructions, or content that
  misrepresents the repository's purpose or safety posture.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or review.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, and any other official channel — and when an individual is representing the
project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Please report security, confidentiality, safety, or advice-boundary concerns using the
[Safety Review issue template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/safety_review.yml) in this repository.
Do not include confidential information, credentials, private documents, client data,
employer data, principal information, or nonpublic investment details in any public issue.
Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith. The maintainer may remove content,
decline contributions, or restrict participation in response to violations.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from common open-source community standards,
simplified for a small, professional project.
